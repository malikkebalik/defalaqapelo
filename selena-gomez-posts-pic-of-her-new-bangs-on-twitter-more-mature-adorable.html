Selena Gomez posts pic of her new bangs on Twitter: more mature &amp; adorable?

July 27, 2024
blog

Here’s a photo of Selena Gomez showing off her aqua-colored bra while leaving Panera Bread, which is a place that she seems to frequent with much abandon, but at least she isn’t being dragged behind her current (and inexplicable) boyfriend, Justin Bieber. In a somewhat riveting seque, Selena’s been messing around with her hair a lot lately. About a month ago, she added some new ombre extensions (which still look pretty dry and frizzy), and now she’s gone and gotten herself some bangs. Now I’m not as anti-bang as Kaiser, and I think they look pretty good on a few select people (not Jessica Biel). In fact, I actually enjoy having bangs myself because they make me look a few years younger than my true age, but I can’t really deal with bangs from a practical standpoint because they can’t be swept into a ponytail at the gym. So I remain bangless, but oddly enough, Selena looks a few years older than her 20-year-old self with her new bangs that she posted a photo of on her Twitter page. What do you think?

Hair issues aside, here’s some pretty bad news from a musical standpoint. Selena is not only (presumably) sleeping with Bieber, but she’s also looking to get involved with him musically; that is, Biebs has allegedly written some “really dope” tunes for her next album. Just great:

The world’s biggest little couple is tempting the fates and taking their romance into the workplace, as Justin Bieber plans to write and produce tracks for his girlfriend Selena Gomez. Gomez previously hinted that her forthcoming album would be more personal, so it only makes sense that she bring in her beau to help paint a more complete picture of the real Selena. Bieber probably felt obligated to contribute anyway, after giving not-girlfriend Carly Rae Jepsen a major career boost (not that Selena needs a boost, with three movies on the way).

Will.i.am, of all people, broke the story. We don’t have many details on the type of song or the lyrical content, but apparently Bieber approached the Black Eyed Pea and wanted him to hear a song he wrote and produced specifically for Selena. According to will.i.am himself, it was “really, really dope.” If only that could describe every office romance.

[From Idolator]

Is this true? I wouldn’t doubt it. This is all Bieb’s way of ensuring that Selena will never dump him. Or at least, she won’t do so until the record drops and the world tour is complete. What is Selena thinking? She must be deaf.
