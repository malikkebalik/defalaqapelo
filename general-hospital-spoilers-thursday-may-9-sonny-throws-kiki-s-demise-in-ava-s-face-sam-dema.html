General Hospital Spoilers Thursday, May 9: Sonny Throws Kikis Demise in Avas Face Sam Dema

July 21, 2024
blog

General Hospital (GH) spoilers for Thursday, May 9, reveal that Blaze aka Allison Rogers (Jacqueline Grace Lopez) will express excitement over her new single and suggest it’s going to be the best one yet, but Natalia Ramirez (Eva LaRue) may be stuck on that guitar solo that she disapproves of.

Even if Blaze gets another guitar player to re-record it, Natalia may argue that it’s still not right.

Since Natalia seems determined to have Blaze’s brother playing on the track, it may not be long until Eloi gets pressured into showing up in Port Charles.

Over with Jordan Ashford (Tanisha Harper), she’ll have good news for Laura Collins (Genie Francis) about the waterfront project that Drew Cain’s (Cameron Mathison) been securing the funding for.

However, Jordan will also offer updates on Sonny Corinthos’ (Maurice Benard) desire to make a charitable donation to the city.

We’ll see Jordan offer a warning of some sort, so Laura may turn against Sonny even more by the end of this conversation.

Next, Sam McCall (Kelly Monaco) will clash with Jason Morgan (Steve Burton) over Danny Morgan (Asher Antonyzyn) again and seek a promise that their son won’t be walking into something dangerous if he hangs around his dad.

During that tense conversation, Jason will tell Sam about his FBI arrangement and may admit he can’t make a real prediction on what’ll go down from here.

That said, Jason will certainly do everything in his power to keep Danny safe.

Over with Ava Jerome (Maura West), she’ll be offended in more ways than one.

Sonny Corinthos (Maurice Benard) will leave Ava feeling snubbed since he’s more interested in cozying up to Natalia than her at this point.

It turns out Sonny will also cross the line by bringing up Kiki Jerome’s (Hayley Erin) demise and somehow insulting Ava.

“How dare you throw my daughter’s murder in my face,” Ava declares angrily in Thursday’s GH preview video.

Back with Natalia, she’ll be eager to score an invitation to Brook Lynn Quartermaine (Amanda Setton) and Harrison Chase’s (Josh Swickard) nuptials, so it looks like Sonny may ask her to be his plus one before the week’s over.

In the meantime, Hamilton Finn (Michael Easton) will provoke Gregory Chase’s (Gregory Harrison) fury when he expresses Chase’s wishes.

Chase doesn’t think Gregory should attend the rehearsal dinner in Coney Island and would rather see his dad rest up for the big day in Port Charles.

That news apparently won’t go over well with Gregory, so he’ll face off with Finn and eventually mock him for lecturing him about proper wedding etiquette.

Gregory may end up reminding Finn of his past mistakes since his son slept with Jackie Templeton (Kim Delaney) the night before her wedding to Gregory.

Finn has already been dealing with plenty of regrets over his past blunders and all the years he lost with his dad, so let’s hope he can smooth things over quickly and avoid wasting precious time.

General Hospital spoilers say Gregory will mostly be upset about the worsening symptoms that he can’t control, so he may settle down and realize that soon enough.
