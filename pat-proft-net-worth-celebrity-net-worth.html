What is Pat Proft's Net Worth?

Pat Proft is an American actor and writer who has a net worth of $3 million. Pat Proft was born in Minnesota in 1947. He moved to Hollywood, California and started working at The Comedy Store after studying at Dudley Riggs' Brave New Workshop. In the late 1960s he performed as a one-man comedy act. He has written for The Jim Stafford Show, The Carol Burnett Show, and Super Dave's Spike Tacular.

Proft has written the movies Police Academy, Bachelor Party, Police Academy 2: Their First Assignment, Real Genius, Police Academy 3: Back in Training, Police Academy 4: Citizens on Patrol, Police Academy 5: Assignment: Miami Beach, Lucky Stiff, The Naked Gun: From the Files of Police Squad!, Police Academy 6: City Under Siege, The Naked Gun 2 1/2 : The Smell of Fear, Hot Shots!, Brain Donors, Hot Shots! Part Deux, Naked Gun 33 1/3: The Final Insult, Police Academy: Mission to Moscow, High School High, Mr. Magoo, Wrongfully Accused, Scary Movie 3, Scary Movie 4, and Scary Movie 5. As an actor Proft starred as Leo Frick in the television series Detective School in 1979. He won a Writers Guild of America Award in 1981 and was nominated for a Primetime Emmy Award in 1977.
