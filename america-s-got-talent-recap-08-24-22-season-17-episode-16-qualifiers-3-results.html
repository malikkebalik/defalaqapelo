Tonight’s on America’s Got Talent episode begins with a recap of last night’s show and Simon wishing everyone well before they went on for their live performances. The following acts are facing elimination tonight, Amanda Mammana, Celia Muñoz, Cline Twins, Funkanometry, Hayden Kristal, Jojo and Bri, MPLUSPLUS, Mia Morris, Nicolas Ribs, Sara James and XOMG POP.

We start with the top five voted on by America.

Hayden Kristal and Cline Twins step forward. Hayden is going through.

Celia Muñoz and Mia Morris step forward. The second act still in it is Celia Muñoz.

XOMG POP, Funkanometry and Amanda Mammana are next to step forward and only one of them will make it to the top 5. Amanda is going to the top 5, the other two are eliminated.

Nicolas Ribs and MPLUSPLUS are the next two acts to find out their fate. America has voted to keep Nicolas in the top 5.

We are down to the last spot in the top 5, Sara James and Jojo and Bri step forward. Sara is in the top 5.

Kody Lee returns to the AGT stage and joins Teddy Swims and Neil Schon to perform Journey’s “Don’t Stop Believing.”

The Top 5 are on stage Hayden Kristal, Celia Muñoz, Amanda Mammana, Nicolas Ribs, Sara James, and two are about to leave the competition.

The Top 3 are Nicolas Ribs, Celia Muñoz and Sara James.

The Top 2 going to the finals is Sara James and Nicolas Ribs.

Eliminated: Cline Twins, Mia Morris, XOMG POP, Funkanometry, MPLUSPLUS, Jojo and Bri, Hayden Kristal, Amanda Mammana and Celia Muñoz.

The top two join Avery Dixon, Chapel Hart, Drake Milligan and Yu Hojin as the finalists with one Wildcard from the judges.

THE END!
