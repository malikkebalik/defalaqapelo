What is Mariana Seoane's Net Worth?

Mariana Seoane is a Mexican actress, model and singer who has a net worth of $8 million. Mariana Alejandra Seoane García (best known as Mariana Seoane) was born June 10, 1976 in Parácuaro, Michoacán, Mexico. As a young child, Seoane already displayed interest in becoming an entertainer. By the time she was a teenager she was singing and acting professionally. She made her acting debut in 1995 on a Televisa telenovela, when she appeared on Retrato de Familia (Family Photo), alongside Alfredo Adame, Helena Rojo and Julio Bracho. In 1996, Seoane played Sandra in Los Hijos de Nadie (Nobody's Children), where she acted with Puerto Rican Osvaldo Ríos. She went on to play roles in a soap opera geared toward teenagers (Cancion de Amor) and several telenovas before taking a two-year hiatus, returning in 1999 to star as Adriana in Amor Gitrano (Gypsy Love).

Seoane then recorded her first CD, which was released during 2004. Sere Una Niña Buena (I'll be a Good Girl), her first album, became a mild success in Seoane's show business career. The album peaked at No. 42 in the Top Latin Album on Billboard and her first single peaked at No. 18 on Hot Latin Tracks. Que no me faltes tu was her second single, which was successful and peaked No. 6 on Hot Latin Tracks. Her second album was released in 2005, La nina buena (The Good Girl) and peaked No. 38 on the Top Latin Albums on Billboard and the first single, Una de dos, reached No. 22 on Hot Latin Tracks; her second single, No vuelvo mas, peaked at No. 42 on Hot Latin Tracks. Most recently, Mariana Seoane starred in Por Ella Soy Eva as the main antagonist in 2012 and in 2013 starred in telenovela La tempestad as the co-main antagonist. In 2014, she was set to play in telenovela Hasta el fin del mundo as the main female antagonist.
