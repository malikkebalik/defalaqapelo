Moon Boots

Recommended Videos

Pete Dougherty, better known as his alias Moon Boots, is one of the hottest artists on the French Express label right now. “These boots were made for hopping around in zero gravity”….that’s also what the light-hearted, airy beats of this producer were made for. Effortlessly happy and upbeat, the Moon Boots vibe is perfect for almost any time night or day. Chill, sexy and always a buzz enhancer, don’t miss this guy in Baltimore.

Tchami

French DJ and producer from Spinnin’ Records, Tchami is one of those guys who almost never misses the mark. He produces tracks that are instantly loved by fans and fellow producers alike and he always puts together a slammin’ mix of tunes for everyone to enjoy. Original productionUntrue and his rework of Move Your Body are two songs to check out to get you acquainted with this hot-as-fire producer.
