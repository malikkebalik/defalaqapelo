‘Vanderpump Rules’ awaited season 10 episode 15 finale debuted on Bravo on May 17 at 9 p.m. ET/PT on Wednesday.

Even though episode 15 is scheduled to air on Bravo, viewers without a standard cable package may still watch the season finale live tonight on Sling, FuboTV or DirecTV Stream.

Peacock will also offer the new episode for streaming. DirecTV and FuboTV Stream’s target audience is anyone seeking a cable subscription substitute.

Ariana’s (Melissa Benoist) nine-year-long boyfriend (Tom Sandoval) gets caught in an affair with one of her closest friends and the show picks back up following this revelation in the season finale. Another bombshell secret that Sandoval is anticipated to reveal is also expected.

Although FuboTV bills itself as a streaming service with a sports-centric focus, its base plan includes more than 100 live channels and costs $74.99 per month after a free trial. These include NBC, TLC, MTV, CBS, ESPN, ABC and other media.

Along with the ability to record your favorite TV shows, FuboTV offers a variety of on-demand television and movies.
