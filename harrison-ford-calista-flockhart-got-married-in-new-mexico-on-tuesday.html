Harrison Ford &amp; Calista Flockhart got married in New Mexico on Tuesday

August 24, 2024
blog

After eight years of hard work, can we really call a woman a golddigger? I don’t think I can (exception: Kate Middleton?) Calista Flockhart put in 8 years with Harrison Ford, and just paid off big-time this week. TMZ was the first to report – with HuffPo confirming – that Harrison and Calista were married in New Mexico on Tuesday. The couple had been engaged since March of 2009. The ceremony was allegedly performed by New Mexico’s governor, Bill Richardson. TMZ thinks the event happened at the governor’s mansion! Fancy.

TMZ has learned Harrison Ford and Calista Flockhart were married yesterday and Governor Bill Richardson of New Mexico performed the ceremony.

We got the tip from Lisa Stanley at K-Earth 101 in L.A. and just confirmed it ourselves.

We’re told the couple was married in the Governor’s mansion in Santa Fe. Their marriage license was filed today in the County Clerk’s Office.

Harrison and Calista have been together for almost 8 years, and got engaged over Valentine’s weekend in 2009.

Harrison is filming “Cowboys and Aliens” in New Mexico right now.

[From TMZ]

Yes, Harrison has been raising Calista’s son Liam like his own, and there’s even been talk that Harrison would legally adopt him? And some sources claimed that Calista wanted to adopt again too. We’ll see. I was just looking them up to see the ages again – Calista turns 46 years old in November, and Harrison turns 68 years old next month. So… no comment. Look, if Harrison is still into it, God bless. Congratulations to the happy family.
